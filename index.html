MaitHacks Rules

To check your innovative mind and coding skills grab the opportunity of MAIT HACKS.
Participants need to register in teams of maximum 4.

ROUND 1

Themes will be declared online on 7th March at 9 pm and participants will get two random keywords generated from the website after 9 pm onwards. The teams would be required to submit their project ideas based on both the keywords provided and submit their ideas before 12 pm of 8th March 2018.
The results of Round 1 will be sent to the shortlisted teams via mail on the same day at 6 pm. Judging will be based on the originality and usefulness of the idea submitted.

Round 2

The Second Round will be an offline round that will be held in the MAIT Campus on 9th March - 9am onwards where participants would have to submit their presentations according to the template given and show the working prototype.



Get ready for prizes worth Rs 1 lakh, Cash prize of Rs 20,000 , internship and incubation offers, Certificates, .tech domains to everyone and much more. Come on guys, do it for the cookies, if nothing else.

Competitive Coding Rules

If you have great logical and problem solving skills,come on! This event could help in your coffee bills.
The event will commence at 9 am on 10th March 2018.

ROUND 1

Round 1 would be a 2 hour problem solving competitive round. Participants can use any language of their choice.

Round 2

Top 8 Participants of Round 1 will compete against each other in a series of knockout rounds.



The winners will be awarded with exciting prizes and internship offers. Top participants will be provided with Certificates of Appreciation.
