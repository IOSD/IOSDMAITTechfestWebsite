Workshop

Love Games?? But don’t know anything about game mechanics or development. Then this is your chance to explore game development and get a head start at the Game Development Workshop organised by IOSD MAIT.
The Game Development Workshop organised by IOSD-MAIT will impart participants with the knowledge of the tools and techniques needed for design and development of games. This intensive 4-6 hours workshop will explore the day-to-day craft of game design through hands-on activities, group discussion, analysis, and critique.
